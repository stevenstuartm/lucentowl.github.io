Lucent Owl is a humble but useful resource for software architects and developers. The name reflects the goal: *lucent*, meaning clear-sighted and illuminating.

The content here comes from practitioners writing about software and system decisions that reveal not just how but also why. The standard is helpfulness, not credentials.

If you have something to contribute, we are open to it.
